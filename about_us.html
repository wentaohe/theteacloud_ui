Welcome to TeaCloud.

Please enjoy the traditional art of making teaware and authentic tea culture crafts.

We are a small ceramic workshop from Jingdezhen, China.

The long history of Jingdezhen ceramics has been very influential in both ancient and modern times. The large number of Jingdezhen ceramic relics excavated in China and around the world, as well as the auctions of Jingdezhen porcelain products in the collecting world, testify to the great historical value of Jingdezhen porcelain.

Known for the rich history and rich resources, the town of Jingdezhen has long inspired artists into ceramic art creation. TeaCloud has partnered with several ceramic design studios and individual artists to share with you the beauty of Jingdezhen teaware.

Our artists use traditional materials and firing techniques to create exquisite and elegant ikebana ceramics and teawares. Each piece in our shop is individually designed and most of the pieces are handmade.
